About Payroll Taxes

Payroll taxes fund Social Security, Medicare, and unemployment insurance programs. Both employers and employees share responsibility for FICA taxes.

  • Social Security: 6.2% each for employer and employee (12.4% total), up to $168,600 wage base (2024)
  • Medicare: 1.45% each (2.9% total), no wage limit
  • Additional Medicare: 0.9% on wages over $200,000 (employee only)
  • FUTA: 6.0% on first $7,000 of wages, reduced to 0.6% with state credit
  • SUTA: State unemployment tax rates vary by state and employer experience
