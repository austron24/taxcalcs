Salary vs Hourly Pay

Understanding how to convert between salary and hourly rates helps you compare job offers.

  • Standard Calculation: Annual salary / 2080 hours = hourly rate
  • 2080 Hours: 40 hours/week x 52 weeks = 2080 work hours/year
  • Benefits Value: Salaried positions often include benefits worth 20-30% of salary
  • Overtime: Hourly workers may earn 1.5x for overtime hours
