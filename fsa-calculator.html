‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my effective tax rate?

Divide your total tax paid by your total taxable income. This gives you the average rate you pay across all tax brackets.

What deductions can reduce my taxes?

Common deductions include mortgage interest, state/local taxes, charitable contributions, and medical expenses above 7.5% of AGI.

When should I use standard vs itemized deductions?

Use itemized deductions when they exceed the standard deduction ($13,850 single, $27,700 married in 2024).
