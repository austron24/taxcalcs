Understanding Sales Commission

Sales commission is compensation based on the amount of sales you generate. Understanding your commission structure helps you set income goals and track performance.

Common Commission Structures

  • Flat Rate - Same percentage for all sales (e.g., 10% on everything)
  • Tiered/Graduated - Higher rates as you sell more (incentivizes higher performance)
  • Draw Against Commission - Base salary advance recovered from future commissions
  • Residual Commission - Ongoing payments for recurring revenue

Tips for Sales Success

  • Understand your commission structure and thresholds
  • Track your progress toward higher commission tiers
  • Focus on high-value deals when possible
  • Build relationships for repeat and referral business
