Startup Cost Planning

Proper startup cost estimation is critical for business success. Underestimating costs is one of the top reasons startups fail.

  • Add 20% Buffer: Always add contingency for unexpected expenses
  • Operating Capital: Plan for 6-12 months before reaching profitability
  • Legal Costs: Include LLC formation, licenses, permits, and contracts
  • Marketing: Budget for customer acquisition costs
  • Funding Sources: Personal savings, loans, investors, crowdfunding
